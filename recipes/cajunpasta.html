Spicy Cajun Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 4 tbsp canola oil
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 3/4 cup panko
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp flour

Cajun Sauce

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Old bay
  • Paprika
  • Cayenna
  • 1 small yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloved, minced
  • 2-3 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 & 1/4 pints heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 box farfalle pasta

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add bell peppers and onion to same skillet, until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and crushed red pepper to skiller and saute 3 minutes.
  4. Add whipped cream and chicken stock.
  5. Simmer until sauce re-heats and thickens slightly, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add 1 cup grated parmesan cheese to sauce, stirring to incorporate.
  7. Season sauce to taste with salt and pepper. Reduce heat to low, simmer; sauce will reduce.
  8. Pound chicken thighs until very thin, the thinner the better.
  9. Mix panko, flour, and parmesan together. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  10. Place milk in dish for dipping.
  11. Dip chicken in panko mixture, then in milk, and then back in panko.
  12. Place in cast iron pan with heated oil and fry at medium high until golden crisp and cooked through.
  13. Add more oil as needed.
  14. Remove and drain chicken, keep warm.
  15. Meanwhile, cook farfalle in large pot of boiled, salted water until al dente.
  16. Save some pasta water, drain remaining water and return to pot.
  17. Add sauce and toss to coat, slowly adding reserved pasta water as needed.
  18. Place pasta with sauce on plate, and top with chicken thighs.
  19. Serve, adding additional parmesan and chili flakes to taste.
